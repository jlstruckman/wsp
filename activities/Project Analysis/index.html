Project Information

Project: Web Redesign Project Analysis

Client: Get Gaming Orlando

Designer: Jenn Struckman

Site Intent

The objective for this site redesign of Get Gaming Orlando is to organize the website so that the content is more focused on the actual overall purpose of the site. The goal for this redesign is not only to make the content more focused but also to correct issues such as a buggy navigation that either jumps around or does not load at all; colors and fonts that are difficult to see and thus making it difficult to read the content; and better choices in overall layout such as a more distinct navigation/header, footer, etc.

Website Keywords

wii repair, ps3 repair, playstation 3 repair orlando, orlando, playstation 3 repair, iphone repair, xbox repair, playstation repair, winter garden fl, game console repair, console repair, gaming

Demographics and Target Audience

While Alexa's page for Get Gaming Orlando was lacking in information, after viewing similar competitors pages who had more information available, this is what I discovered: The average age of the overall audience was either 18-24 or 35-44. The majority were males with either no college or some college education. They were either browsing from school or work. About 85 percent of the viewers were from the United States.

Competitors

After going through the competitor's websites, I believe it is safe to assume that content should be simple and to the point. Everything should be easy on the viewer's eyes and have information redly available to the viewer. Colors and fonts do not need to be flashy but images are useful to explain the purpose of the site.

ubreakifix.com

Alexa Global Traffic Rating: 218,975

The overall design of ubreakifix.com's website is very clean and to the point. The color choice is much easier on the eyes, information is easily accessible, and is extremely organized. it's a very simple design with the main focus being on the purpose of the site but still looks extremely well done and professional.

Geeksquad.com

Alexa Global Traffic Rating: 37,811

The same basic design flow is used with geeksquad.com's website as ubreakifix.com's website. Simple colors, simple menus large banners being the main focal point with easy to find, organized information all throughout the site.

uptechusa.com

Alexa Global Traffic Rating: 18,490,634

Unlike the other sites, uptech's site seems to be horribly outdated and lacking in eye catching design. The colors are almost painful to the viewer's eyes but the overall site is fairly organized, holding all the basic information needed to be useful. I assume the main reason why they have so much traffic is due to the fact that they have been up since 1997.
